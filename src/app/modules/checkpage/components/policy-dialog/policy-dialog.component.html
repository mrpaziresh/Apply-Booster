ApplyBooster Policy and Privacy

Policy

  • Purpose: The purpose of the Online Education Resume Checker with AI is to assist users in evaluating and improving their resumes for educational and professional purposes.
  • Scope: This policy applies to all users accessing the Online Education Resume Checker within the European Union (EU) region.
  • Data Usage: The data provided by users for resume checking will only be used for the intended purpose of evaluating and improving resumes. The data will not be shared with any third parties without explicit consent from the user, except where required by law.
  • Accuracy: While every effort is made to ensure the accuracy of the AI-driven analysis, users should understand that the results provided by the Resume Checker are automated and may not be perfect. Users are encouraged to review and verify the suggestions provided.
  • Security: We employ industry-standard security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
  • User Responsibility: Users are responsible for the accuracy and legality of the information provided in their resumes. The Online Education Resume Checker does not guarantee employment or acceptance into educational programs.
  • Feedback: We welcome feedback from users to improve the functionality and accuracy of the Resume Checker. Users can provide feedback through designated channels provided on the platform.
  • Updates: This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in technology, regulations, or our business practices. Users will be notified of any significant changes to the policy.

Privacy

  • Data Collection: We collect personal data such as name, email address, and resume content provided by users voluntarily for the purpose of resume analysis and improvement.
  • Data Usage: User data is used solely for the purpose of providing resume analysis and improvement suggestions. We do not sell, rent, or lease user data to third parties.
  • Data Retention: User data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. Users can request the deletion of their data at any time.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience and for analytics purposes. Users can control cookie preferences through their browser settings.
  • Third-party Services: We may utilize third-party services for hosting, analytics, or other purposes. These third parties are contractually obligated to handle user data securely and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.
  • Data Transfers: User data may be transferred and stored on servers located outside the EU, but adequate safeguards will be in place to ensure the protection of personal data in accordance with EU data protection laws.
  • User Rights: Users have the right to access, rectify, or delete their personal data. Users can exercise these rights by contacting us through the provided channels.
  • Children's Privacy: The Online Education Resume Checker is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
